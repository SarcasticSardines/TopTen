Top Ten Super Smash Bros Characters Ranked by Final Smashes

By Treece Hillstrom

1.

Mr Game & Watch's Final Smash is one of, if not the most OP Final Smashes in the game. The reason being because he turns himself into a giant octopus. And once it grabs onto an opponent, if flies off-screen and instant kills that oppenent, giving Mr Game & Watch a point.

2.

Princess Zelda's Final Smash is a very powerful and effective attack. She uses her power of the Triforce to suck in an enemy and swallows them up into a Triforce hole (like a black hole) and then, depending on how much damage they have taken beforehand, either fly far across the field or instantly blow up and lose a point.

3.

Pacman's Final Smash can be quiet deadly and can only be dodged by a true joycon master. Otherwise, you'll be his next meal and will turn out as a ghost. Both metaphorically and literally speaking.

4.

Using her witch powers, Bayonetta will slow time down around herself and can freely attack enemies as they pointlessly try to fight back or run away. Unfortunately for them, she can either shoot them or summon limbs of demons to attack them. If the limbs come into contact with an opponent or more, she will summon Gamorrah, a demon, who consume any unfortunate opponent that's in front of it. All you can do is pray that you aren't one of those unlucky opponents.

5.

When transformed, Super Sonic only flies across the screen back and forth. This is still effective, however. The player can move the joycon up or down to make Super Sonic fly across the bottom, middle, or top part of the screen. The only way to avoid this is to either find a way to stay on the top part off-screen or stay on the little pad that brings you back when you get knocked out.


6.

Mewtwo will transform into their Mega Mewtwo Y form before firing a massive orb of darkness, ergo ."Shadow Ball", across the map in the direction in front of them and if it hits any unlucky opponents, they will appear to have a seisure in their head before eventually exploding or being thrown across the map by the attack. You would be wise to stay either behind Mewtwo or jump high and away from them./p>

7.

Olimar will jump into his rocket, which pops up out of nowhere, and fly off into space with the camera following him. While he's off-screen, an army of bulborbs will come out of nowhere and cover the bodom part of the screen, signifying that they are eating the other opponents. A few seconds later, Olimar will come back down and crash his ship, knocking out almost everyone in the vicinity.

8.

Try to stay as far away from Sephiroth and his Odachi as you can. If he gets a hit on you with his blade, he'll trap you inside an illusion. A genjutsu to you Naruto fans. He'll transform into his true form, Safer:Sephiroth. You'll watch as a commet comes hurtling through planets and crash into the sun, creating a supernova that destroys everything. The world included. You'll wake up to find a large amount of damage done to, while your character is dazed, or either gone.

9.

Kazuya's Final Smash is a crazy one. He'll transform into his demon form and fire a laser at anyone in front of him. Once it makes contact with someone, he'll increase his powerband fire multiple lasers at that person and anyone that's either above or below them. You'd be lucky to stay very high/low or even behind Kazuya when that happens.

10.

Samus' Final Smash is simple yet effective. She fires a laser beam from her arm cannon, but it's not just any laser. It's a giant, powerful laser. You better be behind her before she activates her cannon, otherwise you'll be one of the victims that will either be sent flying across the stage or off-screen.
